# Transparency Disclosure

We believe in open source, privacy, and building trust with our community. Here is everything you need to know about how NotesIIIT operates.

## Our Mission

NotesIIIT is a student-driven, open-source initiative designed to democratize access to study materials. Our goal is to create a centralized, collaborative platform where knowledge is shared freely. We are not a commercial entity and do not sell user data.

## Data Collection & Privacy

We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services:

- **Authentication**: We use your GitHub profile only for login verification and basic profile info (name, avatar).
- **Files**: Notes and documents you upload are stored securely and made accessible to the community.
- **Usage Data**: We track basic interactions (views, likes, downloads) to highlight trending content.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use invasive cookies or third-party ad trackers.

## Third-Party Services

1. **Google Gemini**: Used for our AI Study Assistant features. When you chat with a document, the relevant text is processed by Gemini APIs. We do not use your personal data to train their models.
2. **MinIO / S3**: We use S3-compatible object storage (MinIO) to securely store and serve your uploaded PDF files and images.
3. **NextAuth.js**: Handles secure authentication sessions. We do not store passwords; we rely on OAuth providers (GitHub) for identity verification.

## Security

Security is a top priority. We implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to ensure administrative features are protected. File uploads are sanitized and validated. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to our team on GitHub.
